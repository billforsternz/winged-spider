September 29th-30th 2018 - Interschools National Finals, Mt Maunganui.

Organisers Bob and Viv Smith report:

Two hundred and fifty students and at least 80 anxious parents and teachers travelled to Mount Maunganui for NZCF’s annual NZ Interschool Chess Finals on 29th and 30th September. The 60 school teams had all won their regional tournaments or been awarded one of a handful of "wildcard" spots.

The finals were organised and run by at Mt Maunganui College by Bob and Viv Smith, with the help of members of the Mount Chess Club.

Seedings – always a bit of a guess at school level – proved remarkably accurate in the secondary and primary championships, with top seeds Macleans College and Pinehurst School (both from Auckland) convincing winners.

But seventh seeds Berkley Middle School (Hamilton) pulled off an upset in the intermediate division, finishing ahead of more fancied rivals.

For those who didn’t make the prize-list there was always the beach and the sunshine, and ten lucky students even enjoyed free burgers – courtesy of BurgerFuel.

Mt Maunganui RSA also supported the event, lending tables and funding the chess books that went to the libraries of all the place-getting schools.
