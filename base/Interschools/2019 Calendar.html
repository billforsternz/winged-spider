NZCF Interschools
The New Zealand Interschools Championship is organised by the New Zealand Chess Federation. Please note that only official NZCF Interschools Qualifying events (as listed below) will generate qualifiers for the 2019 National Finals to be held in Palmerston North on the weekend of September 28th and 29th.
The qualifying stages are split into 21 geographic regions. In each region the top Primary, Intermediate and Secondary school qualify to play in the National Finals.
The 2019 rules are now available for download here (pdf document).
You can use the logo on entry forms and promotional material: download logo gif here.
The 2019 co-ordinator is Bob Smith, e-mail caissa1530@gmail.com

Regional organisers should email Bob with details of their 2019 calendar entries, for listing here. To assist in organising events, NZCF has produced a new resource – a generic entry form (non-compulsory). Details here, download the form here.
